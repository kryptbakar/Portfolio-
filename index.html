(02) — Approach Methodology

Security, engineered
end to end.

  1. 01

    Model

    Threat-model before a line ships — map the attack surface, the adversaries, and what "secure" has to mean for this system.

  2. 02

    Build

    Engineer secure-by-default: input validation, authenticated flows, least privilege, and parameterized everything.

  3. 03

    Gate

    Shift left. SAST, SCA, and secret-scanning gates that fail the build fast — security as a property of the pipeline.

  4. 04

    Harden

    Pen-test, map controls to OWASP & NIST, and prove it with evidence — not assertions.

022026 · Research

DQN Noise
Allocation

Physical-Layer Wireless Security · RL

A Deep Q-Network that adaptively splits transmit power between signal and artificial jamming noise on a MISO wiretap channel under imperfect CSI. Trained against MRT beamforming and Rayleigh fading, it gains +0.299 bits/s/Hz of secrecy rate over fixed allocation at low SNR — learning to protect the link where classical optimizers fail.
